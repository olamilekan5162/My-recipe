Samosa Spring Rolls

Description

Crispy spring rolls filled wit spiced potatoes, peas, and onions,
served with a side of chutney or ratia

Ingredient

  • Potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • Lemon juice
  • Cup peas
  • Finely choped onions
  • Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Cup of lentis

Steps

  1. Prepare the filling
  2. Thaw the spring rolls pastry
  3. Assemble the rolls
  4. Fry or bake
  5. Serve
