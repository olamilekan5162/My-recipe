Plantain Chips

Description

Thinly sliced plantains fried until crispy,
seasoned with salt and spices, making a delicious snack

Ingredient

  • Ripe plantain
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Pill the plantain
  2. Slice the plantain
  3. Heat the oil
  4. Fry the plantain
  5. Drain excess oil
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Serve
